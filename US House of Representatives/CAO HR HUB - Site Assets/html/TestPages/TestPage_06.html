Member Office Career Paths

The House Human Resources Hub's career paths are designed as a developmental resource specifically for staff within the U.S. House of Representatives. The pathing tools outline the unique roles, responsibilities, skills, sample career options, and associated training opportunities for 16 positions within the Member office community. Each of the 16 positions align within one of four main career paths (Administrative, Communications, District, and Legislative). Committee career paths will be added in the future.

All House staff are invited to use these resources to explore career possibilities and to develop short-and-longer-range individual developmental training plans. To get started, scroll down or use the buttons on the right, to explore specific career paths, view the overall career map, or compare different positions.

Note: The content presented is intended for developmental purposes only. Position titles, paths, and opportunities in individual Member offices may differ.
